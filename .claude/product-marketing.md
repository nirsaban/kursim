# Kursim — Brand & Copy Voice

Reference for anyone (human or AI) writing copy for Kursim. All user-facing app
strings live in `src/lib/he.ts`; course/landing content lives per-tenant in the DB.

## Who we talk to
- **Course creators** (owners): experts who want to sell & deliver their knowledge without babysitting tech or fighting password-sharing.
- **Students**: Israelis learning a skill — want momentum, warmth, and a reason to come back tomorrow.

## Voice: warm & Israeli
Write like a real Israeli mentor talking to a friend — not a corporate brochure, not an AI.

- **Human & spoken.** Natural Hebrew, second person plural, contractions. A well-placed "יאללה" is on-brand. Rhetorical questions are welcome ("אז מה, נצא לדרך?").
- **Warm & encouraging.** Especially in empty states, greetings, and completion moments — celebrate progress, reduce anxiety ("אין לחץ — זה קורה לכולם").
- **Specific over vague.** Concrete outcomes and numbers beat buzzwords. Never "streamline/optimize/innovative".
- **Confident, not hypey.** Drop "מאוד/ממש/כמעט". No exclamation-point spam. Trust > pressure.
- **Honest.** Never fabricate testimonials, stats, or guarantees. Only say what's true.
- **Keep money & security moments calm.** At checkout/trust copy, stay clear and reassuring, ease off the slang.

## CTAs
Action + what they get. Prefer energy: `יאללה, מתחילים`, `יאללה, נרשמים`. Avoid bare `שליחה`/`הרשמה`/`לחץ כאן`.

## Do / Don't
- ✅ "עוד רגע מתמלא כאן — הקורס הראשון שלכם ממש מעבר לפינה"
- ❌ "אין קורסים עדיין."
- ✅ "וואו, סיימתם את הקורס! 🎓"
- ❌ "הקורס הושלם בהצלחה."
